About Harvest Farms

Harvest Farms, established in 2017 and located in Chuka, is a trusted provider of high-quality agricultural products. Specializing in grafted avocado seedlings, macadamia seedlings, and other farm essentials, the company is committed to supporting farmers with reliable solutions for improved yields.

Our Services

High-Quality Seedlings

At Harvest Farms, we are dedicated to providing high-quality grafted avocado seedlings.

Farming Consultation

Our experts offer personalized farming advice to help you maximize yields and adopt sustainable practices.

Nationwide Delivery

At Harvest Farms, we offer nationwide delivery to ensure every farmer, regardless of location, has access to our high-quality agricultural products.

Our Vision

By 2030, Harvest Farms envisions becoming a leading provider of sustainable agricultural solutions across Africa. We aim to empower farmers with high-quality seedlings, innovative farming practices, and eco-friendly products that enhance productivity while preserving the environment. Through our commitment to excellence, community development, and agricultural innovation, we strive to create a lasting impact by fostering food security and improving livelihoods for generations to come.

Our Mission

Harvest Farms' mission is to revolutionize agriculture by providing high-quality grafted avocado and macadamia seedlings, along with innovative farming solutions that empower farmers to achieve sustainable growth. We are dedicated to promoting environmental conservation, enhancing productivity, and supporting local communities through education and access to reliable agricultural resources. By fostering trust, innovation, and excellence, we aim to transform farming practices and contribute to a thriving agricultural ecosystem.
